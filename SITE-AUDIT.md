## 8. Notable Discoveries

1. **WeChat is a channel** — CM contact includes WeChat ID. This should be in the footer/contact page for ZH side. Important for the Chinese-speaking audience.

2. **Bus routes are different per service** — EN has 4 stops (campus only), ZH has 7 stops (includes off-campus apartments). Both need to be surfaced clearly on their respective Sundays pages.

3. **Holiday/summer schedule variations** — Combined EN/ZH service during Cornell breaks. Summer has different times and locations (basement vs main hall). The CMS needs to handle schedule overrides or Sanity siteSettings needs seasonal toggle fields.

4. **Cornell RSO status** — FICCC EM is a registered student org of Cornell. This should be in the footer. It's a trust signal for Cornell students.

5. **EFCA affiliation** — Church is part of the Evangelical Free Church of America. The CM faith statement is the EFCA statement. May want to link to EFCA.

6. **Giving is PayPal only** — No dedicated giving platform (Tithe.ly, Pushpay, etc.). PRD flagged this as an open question. PayPal works for launch.

7. **Sermons are nearly empty on both sites** — EM shows a single embed, CM shows a blog post. The actual sermon archive likely lives on YouTube or a podcast host. This needs clarification before building the sermon library.

8. **Pastor job posting on CM site** — The 教會事工 page is partly a pastoral hiring advertisement. This content is time-sensitive and should not migrate as-is.

9. **The EN vision statement is exceptional** — ~2000 words, deeply personal, theologically grounded, addresses the Cornell student lifecycle directly. This is the kind of content the design language doc says to treat with care ("words are cared for here").
