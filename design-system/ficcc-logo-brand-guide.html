Complete logo system — 46 SVG variants across 5 name configurations, 4 layouts, 2 color modes, and monochrome. Subtitle text sized for legibility.

01 · Name variants
Five text configurations
Every lockup is available in these text options. The abbreviation "FICCC" uses Lora Medium with wide tracking. The full name uses DM Sans Medium at 13px — legible at any reasonable logo size. Bilingual uses the full English name over the Chinese name with no abbreviation.

Construction reference
ElementSpecification
Subtitle textDM Sans Medium (500), 13px in vertical lockups, 11.5–12px in horizontal. Color: #6E675D (base-700) on light, #A8D4BC (primary-200) on dark.
Full name wrappingTwo lines: "First Ithaca Chinese" / "Christian Church". Never on one line — it's too long to read at logo scale.
Bilingual formatFull English name (two lines) over Chinese name. No abbreviation. English in text-primary color, Chinese in text-secondary.
Cross stroke7.5px, round linecap. Vertical: y 34→126. Horizontal: x 88→132, y 59.
Minimum sizesIcon: 16px. Vertical with text: 120px wide. Horizontal: 200px wide.
Clear spaceMinimum padding = circle radius ÷ 4 (~23px) on all sides.
